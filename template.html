Manifesto


When we see evil, we shall do good!

Cities all over the free world have become targets for display of support for Russian fascism, their fascist iconography, their support for killing, torture, rape and genocide, their support for imperialistic conquest, suport for total disregard for human life, human suffering, as well as lives and suffering of other living creatures, pets, farm animals, wildlife.

We, conscious people of the free world, can not stand for that. We must retaliate. Being civilized people, we will not retaliate with violence, nor will we retaliate with virtue signaling displays, mirroring Russian fascist displays. We are going to subvert their fascist displays, and turn them into real, valuable, tangible support for Ukraine's struggle for survival. Every time we see a Russian flag, Russian Z sign or V sign, we are going to donate money to the Armed Forces of Ukraine. The more fascist signs they display, the more financial support Armed Forces of Ukraine are going to receive.

Action plan


When you see a Russia supporting troll doing any of the following:

waving a Russian flag

displaying a Z sign

displaying a V sign

displaying an orange and black ribbon of St George

displaying any kind of moral support to Russian genocidal invasion of Ukraine

Respond with these actions:

snap a picture of the troll

make a donation to the Armed forces of Ukraine

make a screenshot of your donation

cover up your personal information in the donation screenshot

Post the picture of the troll, as well as your donation screenshot (with your personal information obscured) to #SeeEvilDoGood twitter thread, or to SeeEvilDoGood Facebook group

Watch the troll's head explode, as they realize, that their evil Russia supporting virtue signaling has been directly translated into real, valuable, tangible support for the Armed Forces of Ukraine.
